How deep should the extraction go?

For annual reports and transcripts, tables are often the most critical (and hardest) parts to extract accurately.

A

Text-Centric

Focus primarily on the narrative text. Simple table extraction is sufficient.

B

Table-Critical

Tables contain the most important data. We need high-fidelity extraction of numeric data from complex tables.

C

Vision-Enhanced

Extract data from charts, graphs, and complex visual layouts using multimodal models.
