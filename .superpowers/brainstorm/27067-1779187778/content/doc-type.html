What kind of documents are we processing?

Different document types require different extraction and chunking strategies.

1

Structured Documents

Invoices, bank statements, or tax forms. Fixed layouts, heavy on tables and key-value pairs.

2

Unstructured Documents

Annual reports (10-Ks), research papers, or earnings call transcripts. Mostly text with some embedded tables.

3

A Mix of Both

A variety of financial documents with different formats and structures.
